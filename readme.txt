== Frequently Asked Questions ==
Q: Do I need coding knowledge to use this plugin?

No. Video Reviews is designed to be completely user-friendly with an intuitive interface - no coding required.

Q: Is Video Reviews mobile responsive?

Yes. The plugin automatically adjusts to provide optimal viewing experiences across all devices - phones, tablets, and desktops.

Q: Can I use YouTube or Vimeo videos?

Yes. Video Reviews fully supports both YouTube and Vimeo platforms with advanced integration options.

Q: Can I show horizontal videos?

Yes. You can choose between horizontal and vertical layouts to best suit your website's design.
